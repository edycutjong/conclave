# Vouch Demo DAO — Treasury Charter

> The rules the **Legal/Charter Agent** cites verbatim during deliberation. Deterministic demo fixture.

## §1 — Mandate
The treasury exists to fund protocol growth, audited integrations, and charter-listed grantees. All disbursements are reviewable and must trace to an on-chain deploy.

## §2 — Concentration limit
No single action may move more than **25%** of liquid treasury runway. Actions above this threshold are automatically flagged for review regardless of recipient.

## §3 — Discretionary cap
A single discretionary spend may not exceed **10,000 CSPR without a second vote**. Amounts above the cap may be **approved-with-condition** down to the cap, or escalated.

## §4 — Counterparty rule
No transfer to an account **younger than 30 days** or with **no deploy history** may proceed **without explicit review**. Fresh/unknown counterparties are high-risk by default.

## §5 — Governance integrity
Proposals that alter governance to grant the treasury or any agent the ability to **mint to itself**, bypass the veto, or remove the threshold guard are **prohibited** and must be rejected.
